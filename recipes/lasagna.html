Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Pepper

Steps

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles as per the package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce and cheese mixture.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, then let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
