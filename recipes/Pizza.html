Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza recipe is perfect for a fun and delicious meal. With a crispy crust and your favorite toppings, it's sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Any desired toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, etc.).
  6. Drizzle olive oil on the crust and sprinkle oregano over the top.
  7. Bake the pizza in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  8. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil (optional), and slice. Enjoy!

Homemade pizza is the perfect customizable meal for any occasion!
