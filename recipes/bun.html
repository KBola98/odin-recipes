Bun Recipe

Description

This delicious homemade bun recipe is perfect for breakfast or as a snack. Soft, fluffy, and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Water

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast by mixing it with warm water and sugar. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture, melted butter, and milk.
  3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Shape the dough into small buns and place them on a baking sheet.
  6. Let the buns rise for another 30 minutes, then bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your fresh, soft buns with a cup of tea or as a sandwich base!
